About This Event

A Crown Within Reach of Every City

Forever Miss India is unique in its concept: we reach pan India. Several participants register from each city; the top 3 of every city move to the state level, and state winners compete for the national title of Forever Miss India 2026.

The model is built for maximum transparency, and every participant becomes a lifetime member of the FSIA community — gaining grooming, training, media exposure and Google visibility for her profile, whether or not she takes the crown.

You don't need previous pageant experience or a preliminary title — every eligible girl from anywhere in India can register directly.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Unmarried women
  • Age 18 to 35 years
  • Open to every city and state of India
  • No previous pageant experience required
  • Previous FSIA title winners cannot re-enter
